ABOUT

Luxury, Class and Prestige melded perfectly into one, The Sanctum Hotel, Munich is a slice of elegance born from the desire to be the very best. A stunning edifice from top to bottom, it stimulates the human appetite for opulence, housing clients from all over Erde. A touch of excellence within elegance.

ROOMS &
FACILITIES

There are 400 rooms in 5 types ranging from compact semi-double rooms with 16 square meters of space, up to suite rooms with 65 square meters. Our customized interior with all furnishings are hand picked and supervised by Hotel Executives and comfortable and premium mattresses by are used in all rooms.

All rooms are non-smoking, a smoking lounge is provided on each floor of the hotel.
